MISSION

Forden Co. is a company dedicated to designing user-friendly applications for the environment - offering quality technologies and giving utmost importance to accessibility and convenience to our users.

Forden Co. is a technologically centered company dedicated to becoming warriors against environmental degradation and stewards of God’s creation by providing its users with the best opportunity to grow as a person and as a protector of the environment, for the environment.

Unite people under the same cause of protecting and preserving the environment for the upcoming generations

How we were inspired to do what we do

The Community

  • Barangay Lanatan, Balayan, Batangas
  • Population: 2,538 (as of 2020)
  • Area: 41.98 sq. mi.

The Problem

  • Lack of Waste Disposal Options
  • Absence of Garbage Collection Services
  • Growth of Health-Threatening Activities such as Combustion
  • Landfilling in Public and Private Areas
  • Worsening Environmental Negligence

The Causes

  • Deprivation of Concrete Schedule of Waste Collection
  • Sustainable Waste Disposal Illiteracy
  • Adaptation of Combustion as a Form of Trash Elimination

Our solution to the problem; introducing our application:

"TrashBuddy is a multi-platform application that essentially introduces users to sustainable waste disposal options to prevent them from burning trash. After fulfilling the necessary user inputs, the application provides several suggestions for managing the household’s trash with both the users’ and the environment's benefit in mind. Through combining informative and interactive features, complemented by compelling visuals, the application provides both practicality and interest in striving for proper waste management in the community. "

What TrashBuddy can do for you

Virtual Trash Cans

  • Manage and Keep Track of Waste Generation
  • Hassle-free Task
  • Generates Waste Summary to Raise Awareness

Personalized Sustainable Recommendations

  • Showcases Alternative Waste Disposal Methods
  • Uses the Concept of 3Rs and Composting
  • Users Learn Ways to Manage their Trash

Trash Out!

TrashBuddy addresses all five SMART goals

Addresses the 3 (three) Pillars of Sustainability: Environmental, Economical, and Social.

  • Environmental: Becoming Aware of Sustainable Waste Disposal Options
  • Economical: Requires Low Budget; Php 500.00 Capital for the Collector, Revenue Streams - Php 5.00 Request/Pickup Fee, Advertising Sponsorships
  • Social: Promote Social Partnership and Participation

Measurable Objectives; through In-App Data Input and Research Tools and Instruments to be Used

  • In-App Data Input: Basis for the Sustainable Disposal Recommendations and Monitors Waste Generation through Graphs and Statistics
  • Quiz (Mini-Feature): Assesses the Residents’ Level of Knowledge Gained
  • Research Tools and Instruments: Online Survey and Questionnaires: Gathers Quantitative and Qualitative Data to Assess if the Objectives are Met
  • Utilization of Free and Easy Block App Builder
  • Application of Lessons Learned from Work Immersion and Research
  • Tech-Savviness of the Residents of Target Community
  • Partnership with Local Junk Shops

Consequently, the project is attainable because of our group’s camaraderie and teamwork along with how we apply the lessons integrated into DLSL’s curriculum such as the concept of sustainability.

  • Addresses the Problem of the Target Community
  • Enhancing Instead of Making Policies Against Waste Generation
  • Encourages the Community to Practice Sustainable Waste Management and Disposal
  • Reinforces the 3Rs and Compost to Lessen the Citizen’s Waste Generation
  • Provides Personalized Waste Manager
  • Informs Users about the Environment through Fun Activities
  • Introduces Sustainable Waste Disposal Method
  • Summarizes Data Inputs about Trash Generation
  • Users may opt to Schedule Trash Collection
  • Combination of Personalized Tips and Designed Features Specifically made for Users

Lastly, the application is trailblazing as we are aiming to innovate and modernize waste management. In a nutshell, Trashbuddy’s underlying magic lies in the endless possibilities and opportunities in our idea, as it can even be pitched to the government for better local waste management.
